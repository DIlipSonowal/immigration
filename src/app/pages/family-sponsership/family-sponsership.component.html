Family Sponsorship

As mentioned by the minister on 11-Jan-2019 Family reunification is always an immigration priority for the Government of Canada as it supports economic prosperity and strengthens the communities

A large number of Canadians, permanent residents want to bring their loved family members to Canada who are living abroad and Government of Canada is introducing different immigration programs to make this dream come true.

If you are at least 18 years old, meeting minimum eligibility requirements set by the Government of Canada and

  • Canadian citizen or
  • person registered in Canada as an Indian under the Canadian Indian Act or
  • permanent resident of Canada

then you may sponsor certain relatives such as

  • spouseor common law partner or conjugal partner
  • dependent children,
  • parents,
  • grandparents,
  • adopted child and
  • ome relatives (if they meet the criteria set by the Government of Canada).
