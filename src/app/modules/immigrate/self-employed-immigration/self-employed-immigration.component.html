Self Employed Immigration

The Canada Government and also the province of Quebec allows self employed individuals around the globe to avail permanent residence in Canada through the self-employed immigration program.

Given below are few essential requirements for an individual to qualify in the federal self-employed immigration program:

  • He/she should have relevant experience of at least 2 years in five years before application, in the fields of agriculture, arts or athletics. He/she must have participated in a world class event related to cultural activities or athletics or have a farm management experience.
  • The individual must showcase the intention and capability to create his/her own employment and create employment in the field of cultural, artistic or athletic spheres, or boost employment through farm management in Canada.
  • The applicant should demonstrate suitable funds to prove their worth to be self-employed in Canada and contribute to the country’s economic growth while simultaneously meeting their own and accompanies’ settlement needs.

The Quebec self-employed program allows individuals to get permanent residence in Quebec by practicing a trade or profession of their own. This program requires an individual to satisfy below given requirements:

  1. Have two years of experience in the relevant field.
  2. Have net worth of 100000 Canadian dollars and enough settlement funds.
  3. Have the zeal to create employment in Quebec by practicing a profession mentioned in the National Occupation Classification.
